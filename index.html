About Us

Bosch & Botha Safaris is located in the idyllic Eastern Cape Province famous for its unique flora and fauna comprising of the revered fynbos and over 40 species of game available for hunting.

The hosts of your safari are the owners Koos and Francois who are qualified and accomplished Professional Hunters with a combined hunting experience spanning over 28 years. Koos has been in the hunting business for the past 14 years, having started his career in the Free State Province. Francois, similarly started his hunting career 14 years ago in the Eastern Cape Province. Francois and Koos are not only family, business partners and close friends but enjoy a shared passion for wildlife and hunting forming a unique and solid team.

At Bosch and Botha Safaris we aim to take care of you as our own family whilst providing you with both a professional and unforgettable hunting experience tailored to each individual clients bespoke needs and requirements. We look forward to providing you with an experience and hunt of a life time.
